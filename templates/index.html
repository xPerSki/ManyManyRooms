Many Many Rooms

Many Many Rooms is a text based game where you explore, umm, many many rooms?

Firstly, read the instructions and hop into the game!

Prepare

You might need a piece of paper!

Game automatically saves the most important info, but sometimes you may want to write some things down

Gameplay

How does it work?

You will be able to choose between different paths and actions. Choose wisely because you can't go back!

Ending

How do I win?

Your main goal is to find three keys. When you collect all of them, you will be able to unlock the exit door

Stats

- Health
- Damage
- Luck

Calculations

Luck * 5 = %Chance to deal 2x Damage
Health Potion = +5 Health
Damage Potion = +3 Damage
